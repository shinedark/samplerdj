How to play

This is a Music Sampler/Dj tool By Shine Dark. The Sampler works like this:

  • 1. The keys Q throguh P row and A thorugh L of the keyboard let's you trigger a song. If you let go the song stops.
  • 2. If you hold down a key and press space bar the song will play, allowing you to play other songs on top as long as you hold them down.
  • 3.If you hit the space bar again the song playing hold will stop. and becomes avaialbe to do so with another song
  • 4.if you press any key on the row from Z to M it will add a audio effect to the audio
  • 5.To load a song use the search bar and once you find what you are looking for, drag and drop to one of the 19 open samplers
  • 6.To empty the smapler just hit new on the top right
